About

Sound designer,
composer,
producer.

I'm Elue — a sound designer and composer based in Edmonton, Alberta, working primarily with animators and filmmakers who want something that doesn't sound like it was made with a template.

My background is in production, jazz piano, and five years of making electronic music in bedroom studios before I knew what I was doing. I graduated from MacEwan University's music program in 2025. I work in Ableton. I make noise.

I draw in the margins of everything. The doodles and the audio come from the same place — raw, intricate, a little weird. I think that's good.
