About

I'm Elue — a sound designer and composer based in Edmonton, Alberta. I work primarily with animators and filmmakers who want something that doesn't sound like it came from a library.

My background is in production, jazz piano, and making electronic music in bedroom studios since I was twelve. MacEwan University music program, graduated 2025. I work in Ableton. I make noise.

The drawings and the audio come from the same place — raw, intricate, a little weird. I think that's good.
